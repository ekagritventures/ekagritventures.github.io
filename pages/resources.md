# Resources

A collection of useful tools, references, and materials for finance research and analysis.

## Data Sources
- **SEC EDGAR** - [sec.gov/edgar](https://www.sec.gov/edgar) - Company filings and financial reports
- **Yahoo Finance** - [finance.yahoo.com](https://finance.yahoo.com) - Stock data and charts
- **FRED** - [fred.stlouisfed.org](https://fred.stlouisfed.org) - Economic data from Federal Reserve
- **Company Investor Relations** - Direct access to earnings calls, presentations
