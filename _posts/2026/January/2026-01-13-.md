I am working on a tearsheet generator, where you can put your tradebook and it will generate a tearsheet. Tearsheet is a tool used by institutional investors to check the "stock-picking" ability of a fund manager. 

We calculate something know as Time-Weighted Rate of Return. It ignores cash inflows and outflows, and calculates returns based on the return during period of cash in or out.

This is not necessary for a retail investor, but I though it'd be fun to see my stock picking abilities, and turns out I am awful(for now?).
